Model Website


This site is simple and effective. Its layout works on any browser and is not full of excessive formatting. It is easy to navigate with simple links and a simple layout.

Mission Statement

Provide excellent security and privacy advice for the reasonable internet and computer user.

Target Audience

anyone to learn about best practices for security and privacy awareness

Goals of this site:

  1. Promote good privacy and security practices
  2. Provide useful and practical information
  3. Debunk misconceptions about privacy and security

Pages:

> Viruses and phishing

Explain antivirus, best practices to avoid viruses, and phishing

> Passwords

This page will contain information about what passwords do, how to make a good password, how to keep a password secure, and why MFA is critical to authentication.

> Encryption, hashing, and signing

The basis of security for the modern world. how these apply to the web.

> Data security

What are the steps that need to be taken to secure my data on my devices and in the cloud; including data backup

> Privacy

What is privacy, what kind of data are companies farming, social media, and trade-offs

> Crypto

What is crypto and how does it work, crypto security, and crypto privacy.
